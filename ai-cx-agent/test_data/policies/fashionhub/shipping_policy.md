## Delivery Failure

### If You're Not Home:

Our courier partners will attempt delivery **3 times**:
- Attempt 1: Original delivery date
- Attempt 2: Next business day
- Attempt 3: Day after that

### After 3 Failed Attempts:
- Package returns to our warehouse
- You'll be notified via email/SMS
- Contact us within 7 days to rearrange delivery
- No additional charges for re-delivery within 7 days

## Address Change

### Before Shipping:
- Easy! Just contact us immediately
- WhatsApp/Email/Call our support team
- We'll update the address at no extra cost

### After Shipping:
- Unfortunately, address cannot be changed once shipped
- You may refuse delivery and reorder
- Or arrange with courier directly (subject to their policy)
