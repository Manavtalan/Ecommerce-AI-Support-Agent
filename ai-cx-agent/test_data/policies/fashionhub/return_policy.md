## Exchanges

**We prefer exchanges over returns!** Here's why:

- **Faster:** Get the right size/color within 3-5 days
- **Free:** No additional charges for exchange
- **Easy:** Just mention "Exchange" when initiating return

### Exchange Process:
1. Initiate return and mention "Exchange for [size/color]"
2. Subject to stock availability
3. New item shipped as soon as return is picked up
4. No waiting for refund processing!

### Exchange Rules:
- Same product, different size/color: **Free exchange**
- Different product of same value: **Free exchange**
- Different product of higher value: Pay the difference
- Different product of lower value: Refund the difference
